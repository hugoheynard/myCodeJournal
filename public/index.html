A WORD ABOUT THIS JOURNEY

Hey everyone,
I just wanted to share that I've recently ventured into the world of web development, and I'm thrilled, albeit a bit overwhelmed. There's so much to learn, and honestly, I feel like I'm navigating through a sea of code with a compass that I'm still figuring out how to use. The excitement is real, though!
This blog is a real world experiment, the occasion to test and implement the new techs I add to my stack.
Every small victory, every piece of code that finally works, feels like a major achievement. It's like uncovering hidden treasures in the vast landscape of the internet. I may not fully understand what I'm doing just yet, but the journey is what makes it all so fascinating.
If anyone has tips, resources, or just some words of encouragement for a newbie like me, I'm all ears (or in this case, eyes). Let's embark on this coding adventure together!
Cheers,
Hugo
